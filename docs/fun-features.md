## 💡 Philosophy

**Make it:**
- ✅ Fun but not annoying
- ✅ Engaging but not distracting
- ✅ Helpful but not patronizing
- ✅ Delightful but professional
- ✅ Colorful but readable
- ✅ Animated but performant

**Remember:**
Professional users can disable fun features, but everyone appreciates a little joy in their tools! 🎉

The best calculators are the ones people actually enjoy using!
